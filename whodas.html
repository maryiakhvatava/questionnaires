WHODAS 2.0

World Health Organization Disability Assessment Schedule 2.0 - 36-item version

Instructions

This questionnaire asks about difficulties due to health/mental health conditions. Health conditions include diseases or illnesses, other health problems that may be short or long lasting, injuries, mental or emotional problems, and problems with alcohol or drugs.

Think back over the past 30 days and answer these questions thinking about how much difficulty you had doing the following activities.

For each question, please select only one response from:

  • None - No difficulty
  • Mild - Slight difficulty
  • Moderate - Moderate difficulty
  • Severe - Severe difficulty
  • Extreme or cannot do - Extreme difficulty or cannot do at all

Please answer honestly; our research depends on you!

This questionnaire should take approximately 3-5 minutes.
